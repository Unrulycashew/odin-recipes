Baked Chicken

Spiced, baked chicken

This recipe will cover how to prepare and oven-bake chicken breasts.

Ingredients

  • Two chicken breasts
  • Smoked paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne powder

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. With a paper towel, dry both chicken breasts.
  3. Place separated and flat inside of one or several plastic bags.
  4. Tenderize chicken with a mallet to evenly flatten the breasts.
  5. Take the chicken out of the bag and dry rub onto all surfaces the Paprike, Garli powder, Onion powder and Cayenne powder.
  6. Next, line a baking pan with tinfoil and lightly spray with the oil of your choosing.
  7. Place chicken onto the foil, and into the oven.
  8. Cook for 13 minutes, flip the breasts, and cook for an additional 13 minutes.
  9. Ensure the chicken is 165 degrees ore more.
  10. Let cool off and eat.

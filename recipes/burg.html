Hamburgers

Standard Burger

This recipe will cover how to create a standard hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Two bun halves
  • 1/2lb of hamburger
  • Iceburg lettuce

Steps

  1. Put sparce oil on frying pan and place on medium heat.
  2. Flatten your hamburger into a patty shape, compress the middle so the meat does not rise.
  3. Lightly salt and pepper the side on the patty that will be cooked first.
  4. Place patty onto pan spice-side down.
  5. Salt and pepper the bare side as it cooks.
  6. Flip patty every two minutes for 12 minutes (5 total flips)
  7. Once cooked, place patty between the two buns.
